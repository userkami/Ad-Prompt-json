Best Practices for Writing Veo 3 Prompts (camera, lighting, audio)

To help Veo 3 interpret intent cleanly, include short, directive keywords and avoid long paragraphs inside each scene object. Useful keywords:

  • Camera moves: "dolly in", "push in", "over-the-shoulder", "360 pan", "slow zoom".
  • Lighting & style: "soft cinematic light", "studio spotlight", "natural window light", "golden hour".
  • Audio & dialogue: Add short lines like "Actor says: 'Try it now.'" ‚Äî Veo 3 will attempt to lip-sync.

Avoid vague descriptions; instead of "nice lighting", use "soft studio lighting with shallow depth of field".
