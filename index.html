Understanding Cinematic Video Prompts

A great AI video prompt combines technical specifications with creative storytelling elements. The most effective prompts include:

Essential Components:

  1. Description: Clear narrative structure
  2. Style: Visual aesthetics and mood
  3. Camera Movement: Dynamic shot composition
  4. Lighting: Atmospheric quality and focus
  5. Environment: Contextual setting
  6. Elements: Key visual components
